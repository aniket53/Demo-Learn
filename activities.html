Dandeli Activities

Have lots of fun in our place

Discover the thrill of Dandeli's water activities as you immerse yourself in a world of adventure on the Kali River. From heart-pounding river rafting to serene kayaking, Dandeli promises an unforgettable experience for every water sports enthusiast.

rafting

Dandeli river rafting offers an exhilarating ride through the gushing rapids of the Kali River, providing an adrenaline-pumping adventure amidst the picturesque landscapes of North Karnataka. With professional guides and top-notch equipment, this thrilling water activity promises an unforgettable experience for both beginners and seasoned rafters alike.

Kayaking

Dandeli kayaking offers a serene yet thrilling experience, allowing you to paddle through the picturesque river waters, surrounded by the lush greenery of the Western Ghats. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned kayaker, this activity promises an intimate encounter with nature's beauty, making it a must-do adventure in Dandeli.

zipline

Dandeli's exhilarating zipline adventure takes you soaring through the dense canopy of its tropical forests, providing a bird's-eye view of the stunning landscape below. With a series of thrilling zip lines, it's an adrenaline-pumping experience that combines nature's beauty with an unmatched sense of freedom.

zorbing

Dandeli Zorbing offers a unique and exhilarating experience, allowing you to roll and bounce inside an inflatable sphere, as you enjoy panoramic views of the pristine surroundings. This thrilling activity is perfect for adventurers seeking a blend of excitement and breathtaking landscapes in the heart of Dandeli's natural wonders.

jungle safari

Dandeli's jungle safari offers an extraordinary rendezvous with nature, taking you on an exhilarating expedition through dense tropical forests where you can spot elusive wildlife, including the majestic black panther and various endemic species. Led by experienced guides, this safari promises an unforgettable journey into the heart of the wilderness, offering glimpses of Dandeli's rich biodiversity and untamed beauty.

JACUZZI

Nestled within the heart of Dandeli's lush jungle, the captivating Dandeli Jungle Water Jacuzzi awaits. Immerse yourself in the rejuvenating natural Jacuzzi, created by the gushing river, and let the therapeutic waters soothe your senses, offering a unique and refreshing experience amidst nature's embrace.

Syntheri Rocks

Syntheri Rocks in Dandeli stand as a magnificent geological marvel, showcasing towering monolithic limestone structures formed over centuries by the Kaneri River. Surrounded by lush greenery, this natural wonder offers a captivating sight and serves as a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers seeking to witness the beauty of Dandeli's unique rock formations.
