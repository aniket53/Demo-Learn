DANDELI - EMBRACING THE FULLNESS OF EXPERIENCE

Deep Gorges, Enchanting Verdant Forests, Rolling Hills, and Endless Meandering Rivers - If these allure your senses, then Dandeli awaits as an irresistible destination. Situated 125 kilometers away from the renowned tourist spot of Goa, Dandeli emerges as a captivating city nestled within the Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka.

For the adventure enthusiasts, Dandeli presents an ideal haven for holidays and weekend escapes. Whether it's the exhilarating white-water rafting on the Kali River, the thrilling mountain biking amidst hilly terrains, or the chance to encounter majestic tigers and rare avian species, Dandeli offers a myriad of experiences to captivate your soul amidst the cradle of nature.


The Dandeli

Dandeli, its name derived from the legendary Dandakaranya forest in Hindu mythology, also holds another tale of the revered local deity, Dandelappa, who sacrificed his life in loyalty to the Mirashi landlords, bestowing an enduring legacy upon the place.


The Urban Center and Its Population

Situated at an elevation of 1551 feet above sea-level amidst the Western Ghats, Dandeli enjoys ample rainfall, creating a picturesque backdrop. With a population of approximately 53,000 (as per the 2001 census), the city boasts a male majority of 51 percent and a commendable literacy rate of 74 percent, surpassing the national average.

Dandeli serves as a thriving hub for migrant workers hailing from various regions of India, including Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh, drawn to the town's vibrant paper mills that offer livelihood opportunities. The linguistic diversity adds to the city's allure, with Kannada, Hindi, Marathi, and Konkani being widely spoken, earning it the nickname of "Mini India."

Beyond its lush forests and thrilling adventure activities, Dandeli flaunts a rich cultural tapestry with historical landmarks and sacred sites like the Ulavi temple, Supa Hydraulic Dam, ancient Kavla caves, and the captivating Syntheri Rocks. Embrace the chance to create unforgettable memories as you explore this treasure trove of experiences.
